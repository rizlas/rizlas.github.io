About me

As you've probably discovered by now I'm a software developer, but obviously that doesn't say a lot. I've always loved to "play" with eletronic stuff and personal computers and I really enjoy building my own stuff. This attitude led me to get a diploma in ICT and I'm currently completing my bachelor degree in computer science. During my career I met a lot of people sharing my passion for ICT and I was happy to discover that even if we had a common interest we still had unique backgrounds and ideas, and this, as far as I'm concerned, represents the beauty of our job. In brief, I love back-end developing and IoT (altough I never worked on a large IoT project). I don't have a favourite programming language but during the years I developed a passion for C#. I think that's enough for now. If you want to know more you can scroll further to check some of the projects I developed or contributed to, and even further to find a few ways to reach me.
